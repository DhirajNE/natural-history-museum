Exhibition Halls

Permanent Collections

Dinosaur Gallery

Explore the age of dinosaurs with our extensive collection of fossils, including a complete T-Rex skeleton and rare specimens from around the world.

Mineral & Gem Hall

Discover the Earth's treasures with our stunning collection of minerals, crystals, and precious stones from every continent.

Wildlife & Evolution

Journey through evolution with our comprehensive collection of animal specimens, from tiny insects to massive mammals.

Current Exhibits

Step back 20,000 years and meet the giants of the Ice Age. Featuring woolly mammoths, saber-toothed cats, and giant ground sloths.

Running until September 2025

Allow 2-3 hours for a full visit. Audio guides available in English, Welsh, and Spanish. Free Wi-Fi throughout the museum.
